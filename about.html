About Us

A small, focused e-commerce reseller built on trust, discipline, and long-term partnerships.

Luxe Distribution USA exists to give brands and suppliers a clear, straightforward way to work with a marketplace reseller. We focus on buying inventory and reselling it online — primarily on Amazon — with an emphasis on compliance, transparency, and respect for your brand.

Our mission is to responsibly bring quality products to online marketplaces through honest sourcing, disciplined operations, and long-term partnerships with the brands and suppliers who trust us.

We are a U.S.-based e-commerce retail business focused on online marketplaces. Rather than positioning ourselves as a distributor or logistics company, we are honest about our role: we purchase inventory from qualified partners and manage our own marketplace listings and operations. We keep our structure lean and our commitments realistic so we can deliver on what we promise without overstating our capabilities.

We begin every relationship by understanding your products, your brand standards, and any channel boundaries that matter to you. Once there is mutual agreement, we purchase inventory through approved channels and take responsibility for how we list and sell those products online. We prioritize accurate content, adherence to marketplace policies, and a dependable customer experience. Our day-to-day work is built around consistency rather than spectacle, so you can rely on how we show up as a reseller.

Brands and suppliers work with Luxe Distribution USA because we are clear about what we do and what we do not do. We do not promise nationwide distribution, multi-channel rollouts, or complex logistics solutions. Instead, we offer a focused marketplace presence for your products, respect for your brand guidelines and reasonable pricing expectations, clean, compliant listings and operations on Amazon and other marketplaces we use, direct, honest communication rather than overblown claims, and a long-term view centered on sustainable sell-through and brand reputation.

If you’d prefer a measured, trustworthy e-commerce reseller over another generic “distribution” pitch, we’d be glad to talk about whether there’s a fit.
